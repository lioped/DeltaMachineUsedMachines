        About Us

        Delta Machine - Romania is a company with over 25 years of machine tools export activity and it is specialized in the commerce and trade of machine tools, machinery and equipment, new or second hand of Romanian and foreign origin.
        We export second hand machine tools, accessories and spare parts, and/or retrofitted machine tools of Romanian and foreign origin mostly to Europe and Asia countries.

        Our sales staff is fluent in English and German.
        Please take a moment to look over our stock list. We want to be your first choice when buying used machinery. We will be looking forward to receiving your inquiries in the near future.

        Our Services

        We have the great pleasure to present hereunder our range of machine tools, which might be interesting for your future investments:

        1. Brand new machine tools like:
          • Single or double column vertical turning lathe with face plate dia. 1250, 1500, 2000, 2500, 3000, and 4000 mm in conventional execution or with CNC equipment. The machines are manufactured after TITAN licence;
          • Horizontal boring mill with CNC equipment and spindle dia. 125-130 mm (table type) or spindle dia. 160-180 or 200 mm (floor type);
          • Heavy-duty engine lathe in conventional or CNC execution with turning dia. over bed: 1250, 1400, 1600, and 2000 mm and length between centers up to 16 m.
        2. Second hand machine tools, as they are or completely repaired or upgraded, like:
          • Vertical turning lathe
          • Horizontal boring mill
          • Engine lathe
          • Radial drilling machine
          • Cylindrical grinding machine
          • Gear hobbing machines
          • Plano miller (portal milling machine)
          • Universal milling machine
          • Any other kind of machine tools for metal.

        On request, we can present you a List of Second Hand Machine Tools available for sale with photos as well as detailed technical data.
